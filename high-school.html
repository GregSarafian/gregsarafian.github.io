High School

In 2018, I graduated from River Dell Regional High School in Oradell, New Jersey USA. This page serves as my high school portfolio of experiences, activites, and projects that I am most proud of.

Experience

  • Hack River Dell: Co-founder & Director of Public Relations, Sponsorships and Branding

    • June 2016 - January 2018

    • I am a co-founder of Hack River Dell, the first-ever student-run hackathon at my high school and at the time one of only ten high school hackathons affiliated with Major League Hacking in the United States. I was the director of Public Relations, Sponsorships, and Branding for both the inaugural and second-annual event (2016 & 2017). My contributions included gathering sponsors through outreach to private sector, handling miscellaneous logistics, and formulating design and branding for the website, social media, and apparel.

    • Hack River Dell '17 Website
    • Read about Hack River Dell '17

  • River Dell STEMposium: Organizer & Presenter

    • September 2015 - May 2018

    • Along with presenting, I was on the organizing team for River Dell's 4th, 5th, and 6th annual STEMposium (2016-18). My contributions included advertising the event, assisting in website development, and formulating design and branding that is present on the website, posters, and all other event identifiables.

    • September 2016 - June 2018

    • As a peer leader, for which I was selected in my junior year, I aspire to educate other students on teen issues including HIV/AIDS, sexually transmitted infections, teen pregnancy, sexual harassment, homophobia, dating violence and date rape. The aim of peer education is to inform teens on sexual health and reduce risk behaviors.

    • March 2015-18

    • During all four years of high school, I presented at the New Jersey Makers Day, hosted at the Oradell Free Public Library. Using MIT's Scratch, I've introduced elementary school students to the basics of computer programming while engaging children in the excitement and fun of technology.
