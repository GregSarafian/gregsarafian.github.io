About

Hi I'm a senior at River Dell High School in Oradell, New Jersey, USA. I'm passionate about technology, basketball, and I'm a huge Brooklyn Nets fan. I pride myself on my commitment to enabling others in my community to pursue their passions through the events and clubs that I organize.

After graduating high school, I intend to pursue a B.S. in Computer Science at the University of Maryland, College Park. I built this website from scratch using purely HTML5 and CSS3. It is a collection of my projects, activities, and achievements that I am most proud of. Thanks for stopping by!
