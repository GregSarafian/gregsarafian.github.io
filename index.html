About

Hi, I'm Greg! I enjoy playing basketball, programming, spending time with friends, and listening to music. I am committed to helping others and to enabling others in my community to pursue their passions through the programs that I am involved with.

I am currently a full-time student at the University of Maryland, College Park where I am pursuing a B.S. in Computer Science. This website serves as a portfolio of my projects, activities, and achievements that I am most proud of. Thanks for stopping by!

Experience

  • River Dell Technology Department: Assistant IT Technician

    • Summers: 2016, 2017, 2018

    • Over my high school summers, I worked as an Assistant IT Technician at River Dell Regional School District's IT department. My responsibilities included imaging Windows devices, assisting in installing and sustaining network devices, and assembling various accessories throughout the campus.

  • Hack River Dell: Co-founder & Director of Public Relations, Sponsorships and Branding

    • June 2016 - January 2018

    • I am a co-founder of Hack River Dell, the first-ever student-run hackathon at my high school and one of only ten high school hackathons affiliated with Major League Hacking in the United States. I was the director of Public Relations, Sponsorships, and Branding for both the inaugural and second-annual event (2016 & 2017). My contributions included gathering sponsors through outreach to private sector, handling miscellaneous logistics, and formulating design and branding for the website, social media, and apparel.

    • Hack River Dell '17 Website
    • Read about Hack River Dell '17

  • River Dell Board of Education: Student Liaison

    • September 2017 - June 2018

    • I was selected by River Dell faculty as a student liaison to the board of education. At every district board meeting for the 2017-18 school year, I presented the latest accomplishments and concerns of the students. As a representative for the student body, I provided my own insight prior to decisions being made by the board.
