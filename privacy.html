Privacy

When you browse this website, data is collected that helps to better understand user behavior and improve user experience. Using Google Analytics, the data that is collected may not, on its own, result in direct association to any specific individual. Rather, the data collected helps to understand, on a broad scale, who this website's audience is. Examples of the types of data collected include, but are not limited to:

  • Language, zip code, area code, location, and time zone
  • Unique device identifier, operating system, and user agent information

If you have any questions or concerns about how data collection effects you and your privacy, please do not hesitate to contact the developer.
